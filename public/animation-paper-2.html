Stage I  /  Diagnosis
Latent forces from data
Model-form errors are treated as latent forces and jointly inferred with system states via a GP latent-force model.
\[ \eta_j(t)\sim\mathcal{GP}\!\bigl(0,\;\kappa^{(j)}(t-t')\bigr) \]
\[ z(t)=\begin{bmatrix}x(t)\\ \boldsymbol{\eta}(t)\end{bmatrix},\quad \dot z = F_c z + B^{\mathrm{a}}_{uc}\,u + w \]
\[ p\!\left(z_{1:N_t}\,\big|\,\hat{\Theta},\theta,\mathcal{D}_{\text{obs}}\right) \]
Stage II  /  Mapping
A Bayesian net learns p(η | x)
Posterior samples train a BNN that returns a Gaussian over the MFE for any state, with mean and Cholesky-parameterised covariance.
\[ p(\boldsymbol{\eta}\,|\,x,\theta)=\mathcal{N}(\boldsymbol{\mu},\,\boldsymbol{\Sigma}) \]
\[ \boldsymbol{\Sigma}=L\,L^{\!\top} \]
\[ \mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{VI}}=-\mathbb{E}_{q(\theta)}\!\bigl[\log p\bigr]+\mathrm{KL}\bigl[q\,\|\,p\bigr] \]
Stage III  /  Prognosis
Closing the loop without measurements
The BNN supplies pseudo-measurements of the MFE; a Kalman filter on the augmented system propagates uncertainty into the predicted states.
\[ \tilde{\eta}_k\sim\mathcal{N}\!\bigl(\bar{\boldsymbol{\mu}}_\eta(x_{k|k-1}),\,\bar{\boldsymbol{\Sigma}}_\eta(x_{k|k-1})\bigr) \]
\[ K_k=P^{z,-}_k\,\tilde{H}^{\!\top}\!\bigl(\tilde{H}P^{z,-}_k\tilde{H}^{\!\top}+\tilde{R}_k\bigr)^{\!-1} \]
\[ m^{z,+}_k=m^{z,-}_k+K_k\!\bigl(\tilde{\eta}_k-\tilde{H}\,m^{z,-}_k\bigr) \]
Result
Uncertainty-aware state prediction
End-to-end propagation of diagnostic uncertainty through mapping and prognosis, demonstrated on SDOF, MDOF, Bouc-Wen and the Silverbox benchmark.
